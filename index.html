Single Cup
Single Cup is Canada's go to online Keurig site. Built with OpenCart, OpenCart is an open source PHP-based online e-commerce solution. I was part of the team that helped tailor and custom program parts of the site. This included invoicing, custom email templates and generating shipping labels to help Single Cup's warehouse make the shipment process easier.
Single Cup
Bilt Ringette
Bilt Ringette is a ringette site that allows athletes to register and make their schedules online. As a member of this project, I was tasked with helping make administrative work easier. This included custom programming of generating PDFs, invoices, mass excel sheets that could pull in specific data and implementing a mass emailing system to all athletes or parents of particular camps. Setting up camps previously was tedious on their old system and the administrative backend allowed them to quickly add camps by importing Excel spreadsheets.
Bilt Ringette
PetPal

PetPal
